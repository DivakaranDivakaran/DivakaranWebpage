Biography

Assistant Professor at Azim Premji University

I am working at Azim Premji University. Currently, my main focus is undergraduate education. I strive to build effective pedagogy and relationships with students.

My research interests are in the field of geometry and topology (both pure and applied). I have broadly worked in metric geometry, structure of function spaces between products of Riemann surfaces, and Monte-Carlo integration.

I also blog in Malayalam at Manorajyam and in English at 2d-life.
